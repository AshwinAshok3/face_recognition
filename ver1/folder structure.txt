What you want involves 4 distinct problems:

Face detection – find faces in frames

Face alignment – normalize faces

Face embedding extraction – convert face → vector

Face matching – compare vectors at scale

If footage quality is trash → results will be trash. No model can magically identify a blurry pixel blob. Accept that upfront.
